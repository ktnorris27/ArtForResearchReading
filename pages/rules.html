Deadline

All entries must be completed by 26 March 2021 for collection week commencing 29 March 2021.

Theme

Theme for 2020/21 is 'The Environment'.

Categories

  • Artwork from all ages Reception to Year 9 and all SEN categories can be entered.

Size and Medium of Artwork

  • Suggest A4 or A3.
  • Any medium will be accepted as long as it can be photographed well or uploaded easily.

Entry Fee

£2 per picture.

Number of entries

  • One picture per child accompanied by entry fee and Childs first name, school and year.

Submitting entries

To enter, parents need to take a photo of their child's artwork and upload it to the AFRR Facebook page with the child's first name, year group and school and make a donation via our just giving page. It would be great to also post the inspiration for the artwork if that’s possible.

Relevant links are included below:

  • Art For Research Reading Facebook page: https://www.facebook.com/artforresearchreading
  • Just Giving link for AFRR (suggested donation of £2): https://fundraise.cancerresearchuk.org/unite/art-for-research-reading
  • Email address (entries can be sent here if parents do not have access to facebook): artforresearchreading@gmail.com

Winning entries

Depending on the number of entries the Judges will select winners and a public favourite winner will also be selected i.e. the picture with the most likes on Facebook

Condition for entering the competition

Your artwork will be posted on AFRR public facebook page and you agree to donate your artwork to Art For Research Reading for the purpose of raising money for Cancer Research UK. It means that you grant Art For Research Reading the right to:

  • Publish your picture online.
  • TUse photographs or if an upload, the file, and publish these on our website, in the exhibition program and in any competition videos that are produced.
  • Use your picture for any materials produced by AFRR to publicise or to raise money for CRUK.
