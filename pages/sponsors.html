Working in partnership with Art For Research Reading

Local connections and partnerships will be vital to the success of the Art For Research Reading competition and exhibition.

We would therefore love to hear from organisations who would like to support this innovative community project, helping us to raise vital funds for Cancer Research UK's Kids and Teen's campaign.

Some ideas of how organisations can support Art For Research Reading include:

  • Sponsoring a specific aspect of the competition/exhibition (e.g. flyer/programme printing, prizes for winners, exhibition materials such as easels/mounts/frames, etc.).
  • Contacting us to secure advertising space in the Art For Research Reading exhibition programme.
  • Making a donation to Art For Research Reading to maximise the money raised for Cancer Research UK.

If you would like to find out more, please use the form provided on the Contact Us page or email us at ArtForResearchReading@gmail.com.
